About Us

By using the Parent Notification and Announcement we will place messages right on the screen. You can even specify for a message to show to all teachers, or just to a particular teacher or parent. This is perfect for telling everyone about an upcoming event or class change, or to remind a parent or sign a permission slip. It provides a great communication tool to prominently display a message right on the screen.
